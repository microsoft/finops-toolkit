## Problem
`scripts/post-provision.sh` and `scripts/post-provision.ps1` log warnings for failed tool, agent, scheduled task, custom role, and RBAC operations but can still complete successfully. That can produce a success-shaped `azd up` while the SRE Agent is missing required configuration.

## Expected behavior
Required post-provision steps should fail the hook with actionable errors. Optional or privilege-dependent steps should be explicitly modeled and reported, not silently downgraded to warnings that preserve an overall success exit.

## Evidence
Examples observed during evaluation:
- `scripts/post-provision.sh` logs `WARNING: Failed to apply tool`, `WARNING: Failed to apply scheduled task`, and returns success.
- `scripts/post-provision.ps1` logs `WARNING: Failed to apply` scheduled tasks without throwing.
